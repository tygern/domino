Bad Elements of \(D_{ {{- .Rank -}} }\)

An element \(w\) is bad if it is not a commuting product of generators but both \(w\) and \(w^{-1}\) satisfy the right-bad condition. Bad elements are precisely those for which the leading coefficient of the Kazhdan–Lusztig polynomial \(P_{e,w}\) cannot be computed by the standard recursive formula.
